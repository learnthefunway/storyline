About Me

Designing for impact,
not just completion

I'm an Instructional Designer passionate about creating e-learning that is truly effective — training that solves real performance problems and helps learners achieve meaningful outcomes.

I believe good learning design starts long before development, with understanding the business goals, the learner's needs, and the context in which learning will be applied.

My approach is strongly learner-centered: I design from the learner's perspective and data. My goal is to create learning experiences that are purposeful, practical, and impactful.

Gamified Knowledge Reinforcement:
Christmas Dice Game

A gamified trivia experience built in Articulate Storyline to explore variable-based interactivity and game mechanics — without JavaScript.

Context

Created three months into using Storyline to deepen understanding of built-in logic systems — variables, triggers, and layers — while experimenting with gamified knowledge checks.

My Role

  • AI Collaboration: Used ChatGPT as a "Logic Coach" to troubleshoot complex trigger sequences.

Phishing Awareness · Storyline 360

Phishing Awareness Extension:
CEO Scam Simulation

A rapid-response interactive module designed to mitigate financial and data risks posed by emerging SMS phishing threats reported internally.

Context

Employees reported real CEO impersonation scam attempts. The goal was to reinforce awareness and reduce risk of financial loss and data breaches through a risk-free simulation.

My Role
  • Analyzed raw security threat reports to extract behavioral patterns and common "hooks."
  • Architected a decision-based branching scenario simulating a high-pressure fraudulent SMS.
  • Engineered "Immediate Consequence" paths showing the direct result of each learner choice.
  • Used Sora to generate realistic mobile UI visuals, increasing simulation authenticity.
Design Strategy

Applied Scenario-Based Learning (SBL) to move beyond passive recognition of phishing into active behavioral application — building the muscle memory needed to identify and report threats in real-time.

Reduced organizational risk by transforming real phishing threats into an interactive simulation, shifting learner outcomes from passive recognition to active threat mitigation.

Sales Enablement · Rise + Storyline

Sales Enablement Module:
8-Pack MD Core

Interactive sales training module designed to equip sales representatives with core product knowledge and effective positioning strategies.

Context

Sales reps required structured product understanding to communicate value propositions effectively and reduce time-to-competency during onboarding.

My Role
  • Transformed text-based SME documentation into structured SCORM learning modules.
  • Integrated gamified elements to maintain engagement throughout.
  • Chunked dense product information into modular, scannable sections.
  • Used interactions strategically to reinforce differentiation messaging.
Design Decisions

Prioritized high-value selling points over technical density. Modular structure lets reps revisit specific sections — reducing cognitive overload during initial onboarding.

Streamlined the sales onboarding pipeline — reducing time-to-competency for new reps by prioritizing value over technical density.
